About

BCN SKATE SPOTS interactive map is a website aimed for the traditionaly disregarded skater community in Barcelona, skateboarding's meca.

Our goal is to provide skaters with the necessary tools to annonimously search for new places (spots) to skate nearby them. What's more, users can open an interactive panoramic view of the spot.

In addition, the map is mantained by the skater community. Thefore, users can interact with each other by contributing to the site in the following fashion:

  • By evaluating with Like/Dislike the current spots on the map, which can act as a quality guideline while also filtering by automatically deleting spots with bad user reviews. (When Dislikes - Likes > 5)
  • By sharing/creating/uploading NEW SPOTS with their own position, description and panoramic image to the server's REDIS database.

Help

To add a new spot on the map:

  1. Click on "ADD NEW SPOT" on the navbar menu on the left (top if you are on a small device).
  2. Zoom-in on the map and click where you want to place the new spot marker.
  3. Fill the form with the SpotName, SpotDescription, choose a picture to upload and click send.
  4. Voilà!
